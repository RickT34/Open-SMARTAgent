### Reasoning Steps

** Output **
Let the other side lengths be positive integers $a$ and $b$. The surface area is $2(ab+3a+3b)=52$, so $ab+3a+3b=26$. Adding 9 gives $(a+3)(b+3)=35$. The positive factor pair is $5$ and $7$, so $a=2$ and $b=4$. Thus the volume is $2\cdot4\cdot3=24$.
### Final Response
